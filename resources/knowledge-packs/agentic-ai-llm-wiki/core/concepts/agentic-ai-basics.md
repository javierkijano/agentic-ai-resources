# Agentic AI Fundamentals

Agentic AI systems are characterized by their ability to autonomously perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve goals. Key aspects include:

-   **Autonomy:** Operating independently over extended periods.
-   **Perception:** Understanding the environment through data and sensors.
-   **Decision Making:** Choosing actions based on goals, state, and reasoning.
-   **Action Execution:** Interacting with the environment to enact decisions.
-   **Learning & Adaptation:** Evolving behavior based on experience and feedback.

These agents are designed to be more than just tools; they are collaborators that can manage complex tasks and grow in capability.
