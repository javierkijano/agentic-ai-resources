# Nous Research

Nous Research is an organization focused on developing advanced artificial intelligence models and tools, including [[hermes]].

## Key Projects

- **[[hermes]]**: An autonomous AI agent with evolutionary self-improvement capabilities.
- **Hermes Agent Self-Evolution**: A sub-project (`NousResearch/hermes-agent-self-evolution`) that uses DSPy and GEPA to optimize skills, prompts, and code.
- **Nous Portal**: An inference portal that provides access to over 400 models, integrated with the Hermes ecosystem.
