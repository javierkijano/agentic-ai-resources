# FinGPT

## Overview
FinGPT is an open-source Large Language Model (LLM) developed by the AI4Finance Foundation specifically for the financial sector. It was introduced to democratize financial AI by providing a transparent alternative to proprietary models like BloombergGPT.

## Key Features & Differentiators
- **Data-Centric Approach:** Instead of relying on private data silos, FinGPT uses automatic curation pipelines (FinNLP) to harvest and process financial news, filings, and social media from the internet.
- **Lightweight Adaptation:** It utilizes Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) to allow researchers and practitioners to fine-tune the model efficiently without requiring massive computing clusters.
- **Real-Time Integration:** Designed to capture the fast-paced nature of financial markets by continuously updating its knowledge base from real-time internet data.

## Applications
FinGPT is widely used in:
- Automated robo-advising and portfolio management.
- Extracting market sentiment from unstructured text (news, social media) to inform [[algorithmic-trading]] strategies.
- Operating as the analytical engine in [[multi-agent-trading-frameworks]].
